Drag-and-Drop

Through our primary market research, we have noticed that students learning computer science are not getting the resources they need. Code Cubes makes coding seem fun and easy with drag-and-drop code!

3-Dimensional

Virtual Reality provides an added layer of interactivity and immersion that a website or app simply cannot. This makes Code Cubes more hands-on and project-based than our competitors.

Modern Technology

We used Unity and C# to make our vision come to life. Our current platform is the HTC Vive, and we are working on an Oculus Rift prototype. Hand controllers and 360-degree immersion are part of our completely immersive user interface.
